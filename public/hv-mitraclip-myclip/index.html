Who needs MitraClip/MyClip?

These procedures are recommended for patients who:

Have moderate-to-severe mitral regurgitation.

Are high-risk individuals for open-heart surgery, such as elderly or medically fragile patients.

Have continuous symptoms like shortness of breath or fatigue despite taking optimal medications.

Have functional or degenerative mitral regurgitation caused by weak heart muscle or aging-related valve wear.

How It Works, Your MitraClip/MyClip Journey

1

Evaluation

3D Echo and TEE imaging to assess mitral valve leakage.

2

Personalized Planning

The experts at HVE carefully assess your valve anatomy and choose the most suitable clip system (MitraClip/MyClip) to ensure the best possible outcome.

3

Minimally Invasive Procedure

A catheter is inserted through the femoral vein under anaesthesia. Advanced imaging ensures precise placement while monitoring temporary blood flow changes.

4

Quick Recovery

First 1–2 days include close observation for safe recovery. Patients usually ambulate within 24 hours and are discharged in 2–3 days.
