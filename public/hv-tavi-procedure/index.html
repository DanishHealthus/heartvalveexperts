Who needs TAVI?

TAVI is recommended for patients who:

Have severe aortic stenosis (narrowing of the aortic valve).

Experience shortness of breath, chest pain, or fainting spells.

Are at a high-risk for open-heart surgery due to age or other conditions.

Have had previous heart surgeries, making re-surgery risky.
